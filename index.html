Our story

A house built on quiet luxury.

Founded in 2014 by Asmaa Elmeslamany, the house began as a private atelier dressing brides and women of presence across the Gulf.

Today, our pieces travel from Riyadh to Paris — a testament to the universal language of refined, modest elegance.
